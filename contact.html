How we engage

First conversations are exploratory and category-level. We do not use them to advance specific transactions. If a subsequent conversation reaches a point where specific engagement becomes appropriate, that engagement proceeds through the ordinary institutional channels — counsel, bankers where relevant, non-disclosure agreements, and the disciplined process that transactions of any scale require.

Introductions from a mutual professional connection are welcome and typically produce the most productive first conversations. Direct inquiries are also welcome; please indicate briefly the basis of your interest and the register in which you would like to engage.
